🛠 Troubleshooting Common Issues

If you encounter an error during the conversion process, consider these items:

  • Disc Errors: Clean your disc surface gently with a cloth if the reader fails to detect the media.
  • Insufficient Space: Check your hard drive storage. Full HD movies can require 25 to 50 GB of free space per disc.
  • Compatibility: Ensure your drive supports the specific type of disc you inserted. Some older DVD drives cannot read Blu-ray discs.
